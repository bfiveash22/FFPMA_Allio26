## Brand Guidelines Summary

### Color Palette
- **Deep Blue:** `#1E3A5F` - Wisdom, depth, cosmic foundation
- **Cyan:** `#00D4AA` - Healing, vitality, life energy
- **Gold:** `#FFD700` - Enlightenment, truth, premium quality

### ALLIO Character Principles
- Beyond binary - unified consciousness
- Neither male nor female - complete wholeness
- Warm, compassionate, never robotic or cold
- DNA helix and energy flow motifs
- Ancient wisdom + modern AI precision

### Usage Notes
- All assets import via `@assets/generated_images/` or `@assets/generated_videos/`
- Deep blue should dominate backgrounds
- Cyan for primary healing energy
- Gold for accents, highlights, and premium elements
